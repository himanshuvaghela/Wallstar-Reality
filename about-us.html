About Us

Our Team

Wallstar Realty LLP is a venture started by three dynamic veterans in Mumbai Real Estate market who bring in the perfect mélange of business know how, market knowledge and technical expertise. They aim to use their knowledge in helping the real estate market reach newer heights. The primary aim of this company is to help a correct buyer reach his desired property at the best possible price.

Mokshu Zaveri

Is the Co-founder and director of the Morya Group and has executed prestigious residential projects like Morya Regency at Pali Hill Bandra, Morya Fortune at Vile Parle East along with commercial projects Morya Estate, Morya House, Morya Classic and many more projects completed and handed over successfully. Currently he is also working on various under-construction projects. He is known for his dynamic personality as a developer, a planner, an executer, a consultant. His contribution has been throughout from the acquisition of the plot, planning and execution of the project, to complete in all respects including getting occupation certificates and handing over, and marketing. The vast experience and knowledge about the Real Estate market makes him an apt visionary leader.

Kishan Trivedi

Co-founded Wallstar Realty, after creating a name for himself in the industry. With his impeccable communication skills and ultimate knowledge of the real estate space, he has lent his expertise to several projects in the Western Indian zone. In the course of time he has partnered and associated himself with several renowned developers – Rustomjee group, Hiranandani group, Parinee group.etc.

Himanshu Vaghela

Co-founded Wallstar Realty.He has effectively handled a wide array of projects ranging from 15,000 sq. ft. to large scale corporate parks, complexes and establishments and lent his talent for the successful execution of projects in India as well as internationally, like Silicon Valley, USA properties, Aamby Valley City. Working at an individual level for several companies, organization and ventures, he has used his forte of communication, strategic mind and creativity to create strong influence in reaching several projects to their desired outcomes.
